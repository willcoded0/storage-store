Product Summary

The Pantry Decoy is a novelty can-style organizer with a false-bottom compartment sized for small, legal everyday essentials.

  • Digital goal: Sell online
  • Audience: College students, renters, and people in shared spaces
  • eCommerce type: B2C
  • Positioning: Playful storage, not tactical concealment

Suggested Specs

  • Height: 10 in.
  • Compartment depth: 1.5 in.
  • Shell: printed lightweight can-style sleeve
  • Base: twist-off removable compartment
  • Best for: keys, folded cash, earbuds, notes, jewelry

Use Cases

Built for ordinary, lawful storage.

Apartment storage

FAQ

Questions a shopper would ask

Is this a real snack container?

No. It is a novelty storage product designed to resemble a snack can while functioning as a small organizer.

What should it be used for?

Small personal valuables such as earbuds, folded cash, spare keys, notes, or small accessories.

Why does the site avoid “secret” or illegal language?

Because the class project works better as a polished ecommerce concept for personal organization, not as a product for wrongdoing.

How does this page support the assignment?
